2023-10-17

This article was written for my best friend.

I am a person who very easily gets tired. In terms of the neuroscience perspective, an individual should be a smart one cuz long-term potentiation is easy to form and neuro structure can be built easily that is why people get bored after they master the trick.

However, I am not a clever individual, at least not significantly smarter than my peers. Or just in Asia, here is a highly competitive society, half of my classmates and colleagues are “Sing Muk 醒目” than me, rest of them are “Kan Lit” 勤力 than me.

When I get tired of my work, it is an endless negative feedback. I spent all day hanging around, and trying to convince myself I was looking for “inspiration”, I dazed and I called it “contemplate”. However, I fell in a swirl of guilt for wasting time before I fell asleep every night. Such a hard time!

The jargon of this problem in the psychology field is “learned helplessness”. This phenomenon is not only presented in mice trapped in Skinner Box or Morris Water Maze, it happens to us too. The core issue that disturbing you in the unconscious or conscious can not be relieved by the minor tasks you can handle currently.

Let’s straight down to your business. Your appeal is the Permanent Residency, but to achieve that, there is not a test you can attend or prepare. What you need is to find a job, but you are still in school. No matter how hard you work, the GPA can not be exchanged for the promise of your future and you are aware that, that is why you get a feeling of powerlessness.

You are aware of it, deep inside, you want to skip this phase of life and straight down to the real challenges is that right? Move to the next phase, you put your heart into it. You are like a warrior eager for battle and searching for the final monster but don’t know where it is.

We can not discard all imperfections of our life, your problem will fade away by the time surly. Doing something like editing a CV, or networking per se may not bring you a career whereas things like that can relieve your guilt. Besides, always make plan B. Although you may not run a bar or cafe in the future, such a plan makes you confident.
