So… What is Timing?

在我还没有彻底失败之前，网络上流传着一部名为《香港长腿妹大战眼镜妹》的视频，这部剧情精彩程度远超TVB的杰作因其缺乏逻辑的对话与人身攻击而出名。

“你到底识唔识中文啊”
“你知唔知乜嘢叫Timing啊”

由此便引出了我们这篇blog的主题，即，“乜嘢叫Timing”

Timer

According to Collins English Dictionary:

Timing is the skill or action of judging the right moment in a situation or activity at which to do something.

In Godot Engine, timer is a useful node especially when you testing your game, especially when you wanna execute some certain functions or emit signals when it back count to 0 (parameters = “wait_time”).

When the generated timer count to 0, it will continue with certain actions.

Here is a little solution if you plan to generate a timer by script.

在property栏中创建一个选择文件的export var仅限于tscn文件
export(string, FILE, “*.tscn”) var
