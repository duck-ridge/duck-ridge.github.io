Path-finding theory in navigaion2D

2023-05-05

Path finding is one of the most indispensable feature in game making. When you want some specific objects trace after another thing, you need it. There are two more functions make navigation system perfect.

  • Dynamic routine update.
    • Imaging a wolf hunting down a rabbit. When the rabbit take a sudden turn, the wolf simultaneously modify the direction.
  • Avoid obstacle
    • Many people think agent avoid obstacle is nature and right, but no. When the agent through the maze, it just move inside the area that allow it to move.
