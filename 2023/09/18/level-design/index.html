Level design & rhythm setting

由多个渐进的Unit重叠而成，每次完成一个unit就对最终的主线剧情推进一些

  1. 教给玩家一个trick，在安全的地带，玩家可以安全的熟悉这个trick （increasing）
  2. 给玩家一个challenge，只有熟练掌握这个trick才能达到一个目标通关 （peak）
  3. 当玩家克服困难后赋予玩家一个机遇对剩余的小困难形成碾压（decreasing）（缓冲关）

Unit 1

1 - 1 前半程：主角frank 在房间自由移动（trick：move），后半程：被丧尸追杀（challenge：练习移动操作）
1 - 2 前半程： 主角拾取武器一把手枪（trick：shoot and aim），后半程： 主角从丧尸中解救John（challenge：射击）
1 - 3 缓冲关： 手枪无限子弹，护送John离开

Unit 2

2 - 1 前半程： 从John了解到了声控灯的存在（trick：发出声音，激活灯光），后半程：在黑暗的地堡中找到实验室（challenge：照明）
2 - 2 前半程： 从实验室中取得信息后，只要发出声音过大就会惊动了丧尸钻出来，后半程：逃离地堡（challenge：防止过多的声音）
2 - 3 缓冲关：所有灯都打开，不再受声控影响

Unit 3

3 - 1 前半程： 前往敌方老巢（trick： 自动组合的武器），杀进巢穴（challenge：顶着尸潮前进）
3 - 2 前半程：穿梭在黑暗中寻找Boss， 后半程：和boss决斗（大高潮）
3 - 3 缓冲关：带着John的尸体回到一片之前提到过到森林，安葬老朋友（全剧终）

Game Design Thinking!

Before I started making games, as a non-CS student, the biggest problem for me was coding. However, I was wrong. Actually, coding is the most obvious problem, but it is not the biggest one. Later I consulted this question with my friends who major in computer-related fields and the outcome of the interview confirmed my hypothesis. They said Art or Story are the most essential things.

That’s interesting. Countless games have imperfections here or there: nonsense story, strange art, weird game mechanisms, not to mention eternal bugs. However, these defections just can not prevent these games attractive! You might think “OK, these games are attractive because they also have some pros”. I am afraid to reject this opinion.

Last Saturday I visited the Hong Kong Maritime Museum. There is an arcade cabinet decorated with a container on deck B. Players use a console to simulate a crane operator in an arcade. In this game player should place containers on ship deck, if too many containers weigh the ship loses balance or fails to load all containers in 2 minutes, players will lose the game. TBH, that sounds boring. But my mom (a typical Asian parent who is never into video games at all!) and I just can’t quit! She played the game with passion and patience even her ship sank repeatedly. The boy line after her almost protests for her long occupied.

That started my thinking about game-level design. HOW CAN I MAKE IT FUN?

Note

This post is inspired by SteveLee’s video. I will try to learn something special from it. To me, game design is an art of manage code, art, story. Like film, without proper montage, the film would not have soul!
