BrewDay

2022-11-25

Winter is come! Let’s celebrate for the beer season!

Beer and Me in Germany
The great thing is that as an untypical Asian, I am not allergy to ethanol. I’ve been fasinating beer since I first visit Thuringia, Germany in 2017. Back to the good old days, Thuringia is not notorious by xenophobic atmosphere as the media reported. Hanging on the city center, I droped into the sea constitute by the soft drink and alcohol for around one month.

Don’t get me wrong, I am not an alcoholic, I prefer smell the beerand taste the first bite rather than drink it straightly.

After I back to China, I try so hard to search great beers whereas all of them lack of fresh vibe.

Beer and Me in Czech
One year later, I got the chance to visit Palacky Uni, Olomouc, Czech and I jump to the homeland of Pilsner right away.

I have to say the flavor of Pilsner is not what I expect: too bitter. Many years later when I brew beer by myself, that is because hops boiled too long in it and released overdose alpha acids. But it is not bother me at all, I like the clean and light texture of liquid. The hops must be Saaz, the famous icon of Czech beer.

Beer and Me in England
On 2021, I studied in west England. Great, I will give IPA, Porter, Guinness a shot.

From the view of bartender I must be a weirdo: sniff the beer for several minute until it goes flat. It seems like British beercrafter prefer traditional way to brew, no wonder I easily get drunk for only one pint.

Beer and Me in China
I finally have a chance to design my own beer is 2022. By control the ratio of wheat I can craft great beer. Once upon time I use too little water by mistake, whereas the outcome surprised me. The very compressed beer is the most beautiful golden liquid I ever saw on beer. But the liquid looks turbid because of top fermenting yeast. (Although I have plenty of beer, 90% of beer eventually used as hair shampoo.)
